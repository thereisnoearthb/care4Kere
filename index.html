Karnataka's lakes and tanks are vital for people, animals, and nature. The government has proposed the Karnataka Tank Conservation and Development Authority Amendment Bill 2025, but experts say it could harm these water bodies instead of protecting them. 45,000 lakes and 11 crore trees are at stake, along with invaluable infrastructure and countless lives!

Karnataka needs you to Care for Kere!
